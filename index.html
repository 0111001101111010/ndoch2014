Learn

Join us Saturday to learn about how technology can impact the way citizens interact with their communities, and government like never before. Meet citizens from diverse backgrounds, from reporters, developers, designers, community members and civic leaders

Inspire

There will be guest speakers and interactive workshops where you sharpen your mind to be more civically active. How can digitizing information and putting it on the web make it easier for everyone in your city? We will showcase what works in other cities Nationally.

Be Inspired

Action

Roll up your sleeves and start improving your community. Digitize historic information and documents and preserve them for future generations. Make a web map linking all the local places to view public art. Create an app to find recycling locations. The possibilities are endless

Take on Challenges

Civic Innovation...

"Civic hackers" as we think about it for the National Day of Civic Hacking are technologists, civil servants, designers, entrepreneurs, engineers - anybody - who is willing to collaborate with others to create, build, and invent to address challenges relevant to our neighborhoods, our cities, our states and our country.
